Acrylic extension €20:

This is an extension added to the nails to make them longer.Tips are used to extend and acrylic is placed over the extended nail to strengthen and shape the nail. Acrylic is a powder and liquid mixture rather than a gel. It air dries so it does not need to be cured in a lamp. Acrylics last approx.3-4 weeks.They can be soaked off with acetone. They are ideal for nail bitters.
