Gel extensions €25:

This is an extension to make the nail longer.
Tips are user to extend and gel is placed over the extended nail to strengthen and shape.
Gel is cured under a 36 watt UV lamp for 2 minutes.
The shapes for gel extensions include square,square with rounded edges,oval,almond,stiletto,squaletto/coffin edge.
You can get them done as short or as long as you like.
They last approx 2-3 weeks an have to be filed off.



Gel extensions with gelish €35:

Gel extensions are usually done with nail polish however you can get gelish on top which makes the colour last the whole 3 weeks with no chipping.
however you can't change your colour with this option.
